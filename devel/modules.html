class iexfinance.market.DEEP(symbols=None, **kwargs)¶

Bases: iexfinance.market.Market

Class to retrieve DEEP order book data

Real-time depth of book quotations direct from IEX. Returns aggregated size of resting displayed orders at a price and side. Does not indicate the size or number of individual orders at any price level. Non-displayed orders and non-displayed portions of reserve orders are not counted. Also provides last trade price and size information. Routed executions are not reported.

https://iextrading.com/developer/docs/#DEEP
